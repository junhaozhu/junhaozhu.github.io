PROJECT DESCRIPTION
I was asked to research about Blockchain to gain a deeper understanding of this emerging technology and, explore it’s potential to expand its usage in the future.
This project separates into 3 different stages.
1.Research stage focusing on understanding its higher level of human to human interaction through blockchain.
2.Exploration stage concentrate on developing its new usage concentrate on a human to machine interaction.
3.Further development stage focusing on creating a new system that enhances the usability of Blockchain, which focuses on a machine to machine interaction.
