meet Junhao Zhu
Hello, My name is Junhao Zhu, or just call me Junhao.

I was a Communication Design – Advertising Art Direction student at Pratt Institute. Then, I graduated, at May, 2016. I am currently living in Brooklyn, working at CADS and trying to soak up the energy from the City as much as I can. As well as seeking for an advertising agency to put my energy in.

I grew up in China and took college in the U.S. Back in my home, my father always tell me that I have cleverness in trivial matters. The truth is, I do like making small and smart designs, and small thing matters, for example our feelings. Advertising is a big stage for me to preform my talent. I believe what people buying is not just a product, but also smile and spirit.

I can't promise to translate everything from English to Chinese, but I can promise you I can translate a brief into a campaign.
