About Me
I am an advertising art director, design researcher, technophile, and a video gamer. Advertising, technology and video game share a common ground: They are all about the new idea, exploration and most importantly – fun. Their sole purpose in my philosophy is to provide their user the best experience.
Being a type of person who cares about user experience for any product, no matter the content, I am a believer in an impact of detail could have on our audience. It is as influential as implantation. Even a minuscule idea could be deeply embedded in your heart when a designer makes deliberate detail decisions. That is the way for a long-term investment, much beneficial and profitable for both creator and a consumer, rather than causing a short-term burst.
