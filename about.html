ABOUT
I am an advertising art director, technophile and a video gamer. Advertising, technology and video game share a common ground: They are all about new idea, exploration and most importantly – fun to be part of. Their sole purpose in my philosophy, is to provide their user the best experience. (I am a huge friend of user experience.)

Being a type of person who really cares about user experience for any product, no matter the content, I am a believer of impact that detail could have on people. It is as influential as inception. Even a minuscule idea could be deeply embedded into your heart when a designer makes intentional detail decisions. This is the way for a long term investment, much beneficial and profitable for both producer and a consumer, rather than making a short term burst.

*Serious talk ends here, fun begins.
