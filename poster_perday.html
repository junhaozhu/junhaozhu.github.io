ONE-HOUR-POSTER PER DAY FOR 200 DAYS "200-181"
PROJECT DESCRIPTION
One-hour-poster per day for 200 days is a self-motivate project. During work time, we always have to follow our client's need to adjust our design. I need a place to design my own work, no limit just design what I want in order to keep up my creative mind. Every single posters inspired by every little things of my life or my recent interest. The only few limitation of the project is time and space. I have to finish these posters on a 24*36 inch canvas within an hour.
